Why "complete"?

For one qubit the arrow is the entire state: a pure state always sits on the surface (|r‚Éó| = 1), and its direction (Œ∏, œÜ) fixes the two amplitudes. The only thing not drawn is the global phase ‚Äî and that is physically unobservable. Nothing is hidden. Add a second qubit and that stops being true.
